Calorie deficit

A modest deficit plus high protein & steps trims fat while protecting muscle and energy. Crash dieting raises hair loss & cycle risk.

Mistakes to avoid

  • Skipping protein or whole meals
  • All cardio, no strength
  • Ignoring sleep & stress
  • Weighing daily during period week

Quick wins

  • Protein first every meal — palm-sized chicken/daal targets.
  • Steps 8–10k on gym days beat long cardio for you.
  • Tea/coffee: semi-skimmed milk OK — max 2 cups; sweetener vs sugar.

    Fat loss rules

    • Protein + fiber every meal; veggies unlimited.
    • Oil/ghee cap ~1 tsp per meal when cooking.
    • One planned treat beats “all or nothing” binge cycles.

    Consistency

    • Batch cook Sundays — decisions are pre-made.
    • Weigh weekly, same morning routine — not daily in luteal/period.
    • Missed gym → still hit steps & protein.

    Cravings

    • Angry/hangry → eat scheduled snack (fruit/nuts).
    • Sweet after dinner → 1 date + tea or honey in warm water.
    • Night hunger → water wait 10 min, then yoghurt if still hungry.

    Energy & uni days

    • Snack 45 min pre-gym (banana or egg + small roti).
    • Mon/Wed: shorter sessions — consistency > intensity.
    • Sleep 7+ hours whenever possible — hunger hormones calm down.
